Ideology

Party Ideology

The ideology of our party derives from its orientation and social base, which is people-oriented and all-inclusive.

Consequently, the principles of our party shall be humanistic, patriotic, pan-African, and progressive. We are established to promote and defend the rights and welfare of the masses and humanity as a whole. Our goal is to uplift the standard of living, ensure national prosperity and stability, and guarantee equity and justice.

In pursuit of these ideals, our party upholds the principles of democracy, social justice, and national unity. We are committed to defending the rights of the people, ensuring good governance, and fostering economic growth for all.
